Interactive station maps

Find your way through subway stations before you get there.

PlatformPath helps riders explore individual station maps, view platform layouts, and plan step-by-step movement between entrances, stairs, mezzanines, and platforms.

View individual station maps Start by selecting a subway line.
Entrance → Mezzanine → Platform Preview the path through a station one step at a time.

How it works

Select a line, choose a station, then explore the map.

  1. 1

    Pick a subway line

    Start from the line selection page and choose the route you want to inspect.

  2. 2

    Open a station

    Load an individual station diagram with platforms, stairs, and other key nodes.

  3. 3

    Follow the path

    Move through each step while the map shows the correct layer and highlighted location.
